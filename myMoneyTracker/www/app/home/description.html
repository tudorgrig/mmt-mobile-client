Welcome to My Money Tracker app. With this app, you will be able to track your expenses with the ultimate goal: TO SAVE SOME MONEY! CHA-CHING!

Press the Register button to create an account or press Login to enter and start saving some money

My Money Tracker is a personal tool that helps you to better manage your finances. It requires Internet connection in order to synchronize the data between the mobile version of the app and the web client (www.mymoneytracker.net).

We focus on:

1. Security: your data can't be read by anyone. Encryption is love, encryption is life!

2. Ease of use: we wanted to make a tool that is extremely easy to use and intuitive

3. Powerful features
