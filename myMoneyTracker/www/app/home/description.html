What The Accountant stands for ?

  • The Accountant helps you to keep your budget under control.
  • The Accountant can calculate how much you've spent monthly, grouping your expenses by categories or by date.
  • The Accountant helps you establish monthly tresholds for expenses grouped by different categories.
  • The Accountant is free and easy to use.
  • The Accountant offers the possibility to manage your expenses by creating an account that can be used to login into the application everywhere you are, without installing any other application.
  • The Accountant offers a secure connection based on HTTPS protocol.
  • The Accountant encrypts any information for a user, and only the user that have created the information has acces to it.
